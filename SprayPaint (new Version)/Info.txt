What does this mod?

You can find spraycans in some containers:
- 9 colours (black, white, red, blue, green, yellow, orange, violet, cyan)
- plenty of shapes to spray (alphabet, numbers, special characters, special symbols)
- Single and Multiplayer support!
- additional language support: german, polish
